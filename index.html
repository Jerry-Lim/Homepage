I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Economics at The University of Texas at Austin. My research interests include industrial organization, applied econometrics, and health econ. I am particularly interested in the mechanism design and selection market.

My recent work has focused on the cause of moral hazard, examining the effects of market structure on pricing and consumer choice.
